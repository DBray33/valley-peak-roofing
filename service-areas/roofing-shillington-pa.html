Shillington's Weather Challenges

42 Freeze-Thaw Cycles

Temperature ranges from 23°F to 86°F annually with 42 freeze-thaw cycles concentrated February 15 - April 15. Combined with 100+ days below freezing, material expansion and contraction accelerates deterioration.

44.77 Inches Precipitation

Above-average rainfall with 30 inches annual snowfall from November 17 to April 5. August peaks at 3.54 inches, while January brings 7.4 inches of snow creating sustained roof loads.

Recent Storm Activity

July 1, 2025: EF-0 tornado 70 mph winds in adjacent Amity. May 22, 2014: Baseball-sized hail across 75-mile path. 30-35 thunderstorms annually with 63.3 mph peak gusts.
