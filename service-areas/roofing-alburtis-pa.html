Alburtis's Weather Challenges

Extreme Temperature Range

Temperature extremes from record low -15°F (January 21, 1994) to record high 105°F (July 3, 1966) create a 120-degree range that stresses all roofing materials through constant expansion and contraction.

47 Inches Annual Precipitation

Well above the US average of 38 inches, with 20-30 inches of snow annually. Record 24-hour snowfall of 24 inches (February 11, 1983) and 8.71 inches rain (October 8, 2005) test drainage systems.

Recent Severe Events

April 30, 2024: 1.25" hail and 70 mph winds. May 23, 2024: 60 mph gusts causing 25,000 outages. Pennsylvania ranks 8th nationwide for homeowners insurance losses from severe weather.
