Signs You Need A Roof Replacement

Frequent Leaks & Water Damage

  • Persistent Roof Leaks That Lead To Mold & Structural Damage
  • Water Stains On Ceilings Or Walls

Missing or Damaged Shingles

  • Curling, Cracking, Or Missing Shingles
  • Granule Loss On Asphalt Shingles

Sagging or Structural Weakness

  • Roof Deck Appears Uneven Or Sagging
  • Warped Or Soft Spots When Walking On The Roof

High Energy Bills

  • Poor Insulation Due To An Aging Or Damaged Roof
  • Difficulty Maintaining A Consistent Indoor Temperature
