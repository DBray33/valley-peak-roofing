Hellertown's Weather Challenges

Temperature Extremes

From record low -15°F (January 21, 1994) to record high 105°F (July 3, 1966). Typical range of 23°F to 85°F with 42 freeze-thaw cycles annually, most frequent in March, causing material stress.

46-48 Inches Annual Precipitation

Well above the U.S. average of 38 inches, with 24-30 inches of snowfall covering ground 44 days annually. Snow loads reach 25-40 PSF, requiring reinforced structural support and proper drainage.

2024 Storm Activity

Hurricane Debby: 2-4 inches rain, EF1 tornado nearby. April 30: ping pong ball hail, 70 mph winds. FEMA disaster declaration DR-4815-PA with 180+ homes damaged statewide.
