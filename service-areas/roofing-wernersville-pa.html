Wernersville's Weather Challenges

42 Freeze-Thaw Cycles

Temperature fluctuations cause 42 freeze-thaw cycles annually, with repeated expansion and contraction accelerating material deterioration. Combined with 110 freeze-thaw cycles identified in southeastern PA climate data, shingles and flashings fail faster than in stable climates.

44.8 Inches Precipitation

Above-average rainfall with peak from April through August when over 30% of days see measurable rain. February brings peak snowfall at 7.9 inches average, requiring 30 PSF snow load capacity per building codes.

Recent Storm Activity

July 2025: EF-0 tornado Douglassville with 20,000 power outages. 59 tornadoes since 1950 magnitude 2+, with 2024's 30 tornadoes marking active season. 62-63 mph peak gusts at Reading Regional Airport.
