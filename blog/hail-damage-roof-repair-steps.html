What to Do After Suffering Hail Damage to Your Roof: Next Steps

July 9, 2025 Valley Peak Roofing Team Roof Repair

Don’t wait when it comes to hail damage on your roof — time is of the essence. Act promptly and call in an expert to evaluate the damage. This ensures you can take the best course of action for necessary repairs and avoid costly repercussions down the line. If you need roof repair in Bethlehem, PA, sooner is better than later!

Here’s what to do.

1. Inspect Your Roof

To determine if your roof has suffered from hail damage, it’s best to hire a professional roofing contractor in Bethlehem, PA, for an extensive inspection. However, there are some preliminary steps you can take yourself.

Use binoculars to inspect the surface of your roof from the ground, or use a ladder to examine it directly for missing shingle granules or damaged areas. Check your gutters as well — large hailstones can easily crack gutters and downspouts.

2. Contact Your Insurance Provider

Once you’ve identified that your roof has suffered hail damage, it’s time to contact your insurance provider and file a claim. Before speaking to an adjuster, make sure you have all the necessary documents and photos to help prove your case. This includes photos of the hail damage and estimates from reputable contractors for repair costs.

The best roofers in Bethlehem, PA, will help you document the damage, double-checking the adjuster’s assessment for accuracy.

3. Hire a Professional Contractor

Employing a qualified contractor with expertise in repairing hail damage is essential. This expert should have the necessary experience and equipment to restore any harm caused by the hailstorm.

The best roofing in Bethlehem, PA, will always come from a local expert. It’s worth having a reputable Pennsylvania roofer’s number on hand for emergencies like this.

4. Monitor Repairs

Your roofing contractor in Bethlehem, PA, should keep you updated about any obstacles that arise during the repair process. Check in with them regularly to ensure the work stays on timeline and meets high standards of quality. This helps you stay informed and guarantees a job well done!

5. Seek Reimbursement from Your Insurance Provider

Once your roof is fixed, you should be able to get compensated by your insurance company for the cost of repairs.

This process may require patience and more than one submission of documents or proof of payment. Always get a written estimate from your chosen roofers in Bethlehem, PA, so any extra costs are discussed beforehand. That way, you won’t face any surprises later. It is also worth checking if you’re eligible for government support depending on the damage.

Final Thoughts

By acting swiftly, you can ensure that your roof is repaired properly and that you get compensated for the damages caused by a hailstorm in Bethlehem, PA. If you have any questions or need assistance when filing an insurance claim, local contractors can offer you the best roofing in Bethlehem, PA, as well as free advice!
