Nazareth's Weather Challenges

Temperature Extremes

From record low -10°F (January 21, 2025) to record high 105°F. Typical range of 23°F to 84°F with 42 freeze-thaw cycles annually, peaking December through March, causing material stress.

45.35 Inches Annual Precipitation

Well above the U.S. average of 38 inches, with 33.1 inches of snowfall covering ground 30.5 days annually. Snow loads reach 40 PSF, requiring reinforced structural support and proper drainage.

2024-2025 Storm Activity
