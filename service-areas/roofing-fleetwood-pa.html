Fleetwood's Weather Challenges

42 Freeze-Thaw Cycles

Annual cycles with critical period February 15 - April 15. Temperature range from 15°F to 84°F with 100+ days below freezing. Frequent fluctuations around 32°F during transitions create optimal ice dam conditions.

44.77 Inches Precipitation

August peaks at 5.2 inches with 113-123 rain days annually. 30.6 inches snowfall with February bringing 17.5 inches possible. Summer peak brings higher intensity storms with flash flooding risk.

Recent Storm Activity

EF-0 tornado 70 mph winds in Amity Township. January 2024: 50-55 mph snow squalls. May 2024: Level 2 severe risk. Winter brings 10-25 mph winds with 40 mph gusts.
