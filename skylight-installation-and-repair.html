Types of Skylights We Install

Choose the perfect skylight solution for your home or business

Fixed Skylights

Fixed skylights are non-opening windows installed in your roof that provide consistent natural light year-round. These skylights feature a sleek, seamless design that sits flush with your roofline.

How They Work:

Fixed skylights are permanently sealed units that cannot be opened. They consist of a glazed panel set into a frame that's integrated into your roof structure.

Perfect For:

  • Living rooms and hallways where ventilation isn't needed
  • Vaulted ceilings and hard-to-reach areas
  • Homeowners seeking maximum energy efficiency
  • Budget-conscious installations
Most Affordable Maximum Light Leak-Resistant

Venting Skylights

Venting skylights can be opened to allow fresh air circulation while providing natural light. They feature either manual or electric operation and often include screens to keep insects out.

How They Work:

These skylights open like a window, typically hinged at the top. Manual versions use a hand crank or pole, while electric models operate with a wall switch or remote control.

Perfect For:

  • Kitchens and bathrooms where moisture control is important
  • Upper floors that need improved air circulation
  • Homes in moderate climates
  • Creating natural ventilation pathways
Fresh Air Moisture Control Remote Options

Tubular Skylights

Tubular skylights, also known as sun tunnels, capture sunlight through a small dome on your roof and channel it through a highly reflective tube to brighten interior spaces.

How They Work:

A clear dome on the roof captures sunlight and directs it through a reflective tube (10-21 inches in diameter) that runs through your attic to a diffuser lens in your ceiling.

Perfect For:

  • Small spaces like closets, hallways, and bathrooms
  • Rooms with attic space above
  • Areas where traditional skylights won't fit
  • Minimizing heat gain/loss
Space-Saving Energy Efficient Easy Install

Our Skylight Installation Process

We've perfected our installation process to ensure minimal disruption to your home while delivering exceptional results. Every skylight installation follows our proven 4-step process.

1

Consultation & Site Assessment

We evaluate your roof structure, measure the installation area, and help you choose the perfect skylight type and size for your space and budget.

2

Precision Roof Opening

Our certified installers carefully cut the roof opening, ensuring proper sizing while maintaining your roof's structural integrity.

3

Skylight Installation & Sealing

We install the skylight with proper flashing, weatherproofing, and insulation to ensure zero leaks and maximum energy efficiency.

4

Interior Finishing & Cleanup

We complete the interior light shaft, add finishing touches, test the installation, and leave your home spotless with your new skylight ready to enjoy.
