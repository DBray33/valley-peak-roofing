Why Asphalt Shingles Are the Best Choice for Your Roof

July 9, 2025 Valley Peak Roofing Team Roofing Materials

Good old asphalt shingles still offer the best roofing in Bethlehem, PA — but what is it about them that roofers love so much? Here’s a quick breakdown of what makes asphalt the top choice for your roof and what you can expect when you have a shingle roof installed.

1. Cheap

Installing a roof is expensive, and homeowners want to save wherever they can. Any roofing contractor in Bethlehem, PA, will tell you that asphalt is their best-selling product for this reason. It’s also very easy to source, meaning roofers incur fewer costs when acquiring materials. Contractors can purchase high-quality shingles from top manufacturers in bulk because there’s always another project around the corner. Its widespread availability also means it’s cheaper to repair — you won’t have to pay through the nose if you need a bit of roofing work done.

2. Easy to Install

Asphalt is bread and butter for roofers in Bethlehem, PA — it’s the material most contractors learn with. Any trustworthy contractor knows exactly what they’re doing with asphalt, so you can rest assured you’ll get a great job if you work with a reputable company. Roof repair in Bethlehem, PA, can often be completed in an afternoon because replacing a few shingles is literally all in a day’s work. It saves time and money for both contractors and homeowners alike.

3. Lightweight

Some materials (like slate tiles) are prized for their longevity, but with that come two burdens: they’re incredibly expensive (often upward of 5x the price of asphalt) and they put a heavy load on your home. Not all decking can support heavier materials, meaning you may need extra reinforcement, adding to costs. Your roofing contractor in Bethlehem, PA, will make recommendations based on the most cost-effective solution for your home — most often, that means asphalt.

4. Durable

High-quality asphalt shingles make for the best roofing in Bethlehem, PA, because they handle all kinds of weather. Pennsylvania can experience scorching heat, frigid winters, and heavy rain — asphalt shingles from top manufacturers like Owens Corning stand up to it all. They also provide great insulation properties, helping to reduce energy bills.

5. Long-Lasting

Top manufacturers like Owens Corning produce shingles built to last upward of 25 years. They’re a fantastic investment for your home and, combined with their affordability, make them the leading choice for roofing in Bethlehem, PA.

6. Customizable

Want to make your roofing in Bethlehem, PA, stand out? Asphalt shingles come in a huge variety of colors and styles. It’s an easy, cost-effective way to give your home a custom, attractive look.

Final Thoughts

Asphalt will likely remain the #1 choice for homeowners and roofers in Bethlehem, PA, for a long time. Its low cost, long-lasting durability, and classic aesthetic set it apart from alternative roofing materials — it’s definitely our recommendation for your home.
