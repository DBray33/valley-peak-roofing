Birdsboro's Weather Challenges

110 Freeze-Thaw Cycles

Highest in southeastern PA with 110 annual temperature crossings of 32°F causing extreme thermal expansion and contraction. Temperature range from 22°F to 86°F creates 64-degree variation accelerating material fatigue.

46 Inches Precipitation

Above-average rainfall with 30-35 thunderstorms yearly. Average 30 inches snowfall requiring 30 PSF minimum roof loads. September 1987 flood brought 6.25 inches in one day causing $5 million damage.

Recent Storm Activity

July 2025: EF0 tornado 70 mph in Amity. April 2023: EF-1 tornado 90 mph western Berks. Wind patterns 5-10 mph average with river valley channeling effects creating variable conditions.
