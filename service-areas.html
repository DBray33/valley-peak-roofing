Architectural Shingles

Also known as dimensional or laminate shingles, architectural shingles are a premium roofing option that offers:

  • Enhanced Durability: Multi-layered construction provides better protection against wind and weather
  • Aesthetic Appeal: Dimensional appearance mimics the look of wood shakes or slate
  • Longer Lifespan: Typically last 30-50 years with proper maintenance
  • Better Warranties: Often come with extended manufacturer warranties
  • Increased Home Value: Premium appearance can boost curb appeal and resale value

Available in a wide range of colors and styles to complement any home architecture.

Metal Roofing Systems

Metal roofing is an excellent investment for homeowners seeking maximum durability and energy efficiency:

  • Exceptional Longevity: Can last 50-70 years or more
  • Energy Efficient: Reflects solar heat, reducing cooling costs by 10-25%
  • Weather Resistant: Withstands winds up to 140 mph
  • Fire Resistant: Class A fire rating for maximum safety
  • Eco-Friendly: Made from recycled materials and fully recyclable
  • Low Maintenance: Minimal upkeep required throughout its lifespan

Available in standing seam, metal shingles, and corrugated styles with various color options.

Skylight Installation & Replacement

Transform your living spaces with natural light through professional skylight installation:

  • Natural Lighting: Reduce electricity costs while brightening your home
  • Ventilation Options: Venting skylights improve air circulation
  • Energy Efficient: Modern skylights with Low-E coatings and insulated glass
  • Leak-Free Installation: Professional flashing and weatherproofing
  • Various Styles: Fixed, venting, tubular, and custom sizes available
  • Smart Features: Rain sensors and remote controls for convenience

We handle both new installations and replacements of existing skylights with guaranteed leak-free performance.
