Bangor's Weather Challenges

Temperature Extremes

Winter lows average 20°F while summer highs reach 83°F, rarely below 6°F or above 90°F. With 40-60 freeze-thaw cycles annually during spring and fall, material stress through expansion and contraction significantly impacts roofing integrity.

36-44 Inches Precipitation

September peaks at 3.8 inches while 25-30 inches of snowfall concentrate from November to April. January alone brings 9.8 inches, creating sustained roof loads and ice dam conditions.

May 2025 Tornado Impact

The EF-1 tornado on May 6, 2025 traveled 1.5 miles with 80-90 mph winds, 100-yard width. Damage concentrated along Route 191, with roof tiles found quarter-mile away.
