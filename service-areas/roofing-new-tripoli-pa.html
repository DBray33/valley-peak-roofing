New Tripoli's Weather Challenges

Temperature Extremes

Temperature range from 23°F to 86°F with 12 days above 90°F and 5.9 days at or below 0°F annually. 50-90 freeze-thaw cycles create constant expansion and contraction stress on all roofing materials.

45.35 Inches Annual Precipitation

Above Pennsylvania's 42.6-inch average across 145.1 days, with 13.82 inches of snow annually. Ground snow loads reach 30 pounds per square foot, requiring reinforced structural support for all roofing systems.

Recent Severe Events

April 30, 2024: 1.25" hail and 70 mph winds with ping-pong ball sized hail in some areas. May 2025: Level 2 severe weather risk with damaging winds, heavy rain, and isolated tornado possibilities.
