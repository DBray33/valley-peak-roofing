Stroudsburg's Weather Challenges

40-60 Freeze-Thaw Cycles

Annual cycles with cold season December 2 - March 9. Temperature range from 22°F to 82°F with 5-month snowy period. January averages 22°F-36°F creating optimal ice dam conditions throughout winter months.

32.24 Inches Precipitation

September peaks at 5.30 inches with 161.8 rain days annually. 19.76 inches snowfall across 38.6 snow days with February bringing 8.7 inches average. Peak wet season April 10 - August 18 with 87% forest coverage creating moss/algae conditions.

Recent Storm Activity

Hurricane Debby remnants August 2024 flooding. June 2020 Pennsylvania Derecho extensive damage. Tropical Storm Ophelia September 2023. Wind speeds average 5.1-6.6 mph with west winds 43% frequency July, north winds 32% March-April.
