Coopersburg's Weather Challenges

40-60 Freeze-Thaw Cycles

Temperature extremes from winter lows near 8°F to summer highs exceeding 93°F create constant expansion and contraction stress. The valley position at 472 feet elevation between Blue Mountain and South Mountain intensifies temperature inversions.

47 Inches Precipitation

Significant moisture exposure with 112 days of measurable precipitation annually. Recent events include 1.15 inches in a single April 2024 storm, plus 20-30 inches of snow creating persistent moisture challenges.

Severe Storm Escalation

April 2024 brought ping pong ball-sized hail with 70 MPH winds. May 2024 storms caused 25,000 power outages. April 2025's bow echo exceeded 80 MPH, demonstrating increasing severe weather frequency.
