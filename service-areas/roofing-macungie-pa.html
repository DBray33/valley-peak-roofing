Macungie's Extreme Weather Challenges

Temperature Extremes

Winter lows of 23°F to summer peaks of 86°F create thermal expansion and contraction that tests every roofing component, accelerating material fatigue and seal failures.

33.15 Inches Annual Precipitation

With 31.2 days of snowfall and a 4.7-month snow season, plus 30-35 thunderstorms annually, proper drainage and weather-resistant materials are essential.

Severe Storm Damage

Documented wind speeds up to 70 mph and hail reaching 1.25 inches during severe weather, including historic events like the 1984 tornado outbreak.
