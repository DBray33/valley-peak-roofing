Kutztown's Weather Challenges

35-45 Freeze-Thaw Cycles

Temperature crossings of 32°F occur 35-45 times annually, most frequently during transition seasons. This repeated expansion and contraction accelerates material deterioration, particularly affecting flashings and sealants during critical periods.

47.7 Inches Precipitation

Uneven distribution with September's 4.78 inches marking the wettest month. February brings 7.7 inches snowfall on average, with total annual snowfall of 25-30 inches requiring proper load calculations.

Recent Storm Activity

May 2024: EF2 tornado 111-135 mph in Caernarvon. August 2024: 63.3 mph straight-line winds. Multiple 3.83 inch flash floods testing drainage systems throughout Berks County.
