Why Choose James Hardie Fiber Cement Siding?

James Hardie is the #1 brand of siding in America, trusted by homeowners and contractors for over 135 years. Engineered specifically for climate conditions, James Hardie fiber cement siding delivers unmatched performance and beauty.

ColorPlus Technology

James Hardie's exclusive ColorPlus Technology provides a baked-on finish that resists fading, cracking, and chipping for up to 15 years. Choose from a curated palette of beautiful, fade-resistant colors that maintain their vibrancy through Pennsylvania's harshest weather.

Unmatched Durability & Weather Resistance

Engineered to withstand extreme temperatures, heavy rain, snow, and high winds, James Hardie siding is built to last. Unlike vinyl siding that cracks in cold or wood that warps and rots, fiber cement maintains its structural integrity through decades of Pennsylvania weather.

Fire-Resistant Protection

James Hardie fiber cement siding is non-combustible and won't ignite when exposed to direct flame. With a Class A fire rating, it provides superior protection for your home and may even lower your homeowner's insurance premiums.

Low Maintenance & Long-Lasting

Say goodbye to constant repainting, staining, and repairs. James Hardie siding won't rot, warp, or attract pests like wood, and it won't crack or fade like vinyl. A simple rinse with a garden hose keeps your siding looking beautiful for decades.

Authentic Wood Beauty Without the Hassle

James Hardie siding captures the authentic look and texture of real wood with deep grain patterns and rich color. Get the timeless beauty of wood siding without the constant maintenance, warping, or rotting that comes with natural materials.

30-Year Non-Prorated Warranty

James Hardie backs their products with a comprehensive 30-year non-prorated warranty on materials. As a James Hardie Preferred Contractor, Valley Peak Roofing provides enhanced warranty protection, ensuring your investment is fully covered.

ColorPlus Technology: Factory-Applied, Fade-Resistant Color

Unlike traditional paint that's applied on-site, James Hardie's ColorPlus Technology is baked on in a controlled factory environment for superior durability and consistency.

1

Multi-Coat Application Process

Each ColorPlus board receives multiple coats of proprietary paint in a climate-controlled factory, ensuring even coverage and optimal adhesion.

2

Baked-On for Durability

The finish is baked on at high temperatures, creating a tight molecular bond that resists cracking, chipping, and peeling better than field-applied paint.

3

Fade-Resistant Pigments

Advanced UV-resistant pigments maintain vibrant color for up to 15 years, significantly outlasting traditional painted siding.

4

15-Year Warranty

ColorPlus comes with a 15-year limited warranty against peeling, cracking, and chipping—proof of its superior quality and performance.
